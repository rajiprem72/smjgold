Leadership

Meet Shri Ponniah (SMJ Mari)

Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers is ably administered by Shri Ponniah, who is fondly known among customers, friends, and business associates as SMJ Mari. With more than twenty years of valuable experience in the jewellery industry, he has earned the trust and respect of people from different walks of life. His journey in the jewellery business began many years ago, where he worked as a partner and gained deep knowledge about the trade, customer preferences, and the changing trends in the jewellery market.

After spending several years learning every aspect of the business, Shri Ponniah started Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers with the dream of offering quality jewellery and honest service to customers. Today, the showroom has successfully completed seven years and has become a trusted name in Tirunelveli. Under his leadership, Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers has steadily grown and continues to serve customers with dedication, professionalism, and a strong commitment to quality.

Over the years, Shri Ponniah has mastered both Business-to-Business (B2B) and Business-to-Customer (B2C) operations. His extensive knowledge of wholesale trading enables him to work closely with other jewellery businesses and retailers, while his experience in retail sales helps him understand the expectations of individual customers. This rare combination of expertise allows Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers to cater to the needs of both business partners and families looking for beautiful jewellery collections.

One of Shri Ponniah's greatest strengths is his ability to understand the unique requirements of every customer. He believes that purchasing jewellery is not merely a transaction but an important and memorable occasion in a person's life. Whether a customer is looking for a traditional design, a modern collection, or a completely customized piece of jewellery, he patiently listens to their expectations and offers the most suitable solution.

His customer-friendly approach, combined with years of practical experience, has helped Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers build long-lasting relationships with customers. By focusing on quality, trust, and customer satisfaction, Shri Ponniah continues to guide the company towards greater success while ensuring that every customer leaves the showroom with confidence and happiness.
