About Us

Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers

“தரமே எங்கள் தாரக மந்திரம்”
“Quality is our motto.”

Established over seven years ago, Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers has grown into one of the trusted names in Tirunelveli's jewellery industry. The showroom specializes in wholesale supply, custom-made jewellery, and retail sales, serving customers with an unwavering commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

The organization is ably administered by Shri Ponniah, fondly known as SMJ Mari, whose vision and dedication have helped build lasting relationships with customers and business partners alike.

Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers is conveniently located at:

Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers
97, W Car Street,
Tirunelveli Junction,
Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu – 627006.

B2B – Business to Business

Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers is a leading wholesale and order supplier, catering to jewellery shops, resellers, and business establishments. Through its B2B operations, the company provides high-quality jewellery in bulk quantities while ensuring competitive pricing and dependable service.

B2C – Business to Customer

In addition to wholesale operations, Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers also serves individual customers through its retail showroom. Customers can explore a wide range of jewellery collections and enjoy personalized service tailored to their needs and preferences.

Looking for a unique jewellery design? Simply bring a photograph or reference image, and the skilled artisans at Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers will transform your vision into reality with exceptional craftsmanship.

Customers can confidently buy, sell, and exchange gold at Shiva Mahajothi Jewellers, with every transaction carried out transparently and professionally.

The showroom also offers a specially designed Gold Savings Scheme, enabling middle-class families to save systematically over a fixed period and fulfil their dream of owning gold jewellery. The scheme provides customers with a convenient and disciplined way to plan their future purchases.
